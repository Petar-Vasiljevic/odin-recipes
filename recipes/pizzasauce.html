Pizza Sauce Recipe

Description

A simple and easy pizza sauce. No cooking and quick to make.

Prep Time: 5 mins

Total Time: 5 mins

Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • Tomato sauce: 1 (15 ounce) can
  • Tomato paste: 1 (6 ounce) can
  • Ground oregano: 1 tablespoon
  • Dried minced garlic: 1 Â½ teaspoons
  • Ground paprika: 1 teaspoon

Steps

  1. Gather the ingredients


  2. Mix together tomato sauce and tomato paste in a medium bowl until smooth.


  3. Stir in oregano, garlic, and paprika to pizza sauce mixture.


  4. Enjoy!
