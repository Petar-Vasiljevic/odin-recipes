Pancakes Recipe

Description

This quick and easy pancake recipe will knock you off your feet!

Prep Time: 10 mins

Cook Time: 10 mins

Total Time: 20 mins

Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour: 1 cup
  • White sugar: 2 tablespoons
  • Baking powder: 2 teaspoons
  • Salt: Â½ teaspoon, or to taste
  • Milk: 1 cup
  • Egg: 1 large egg, beaten

Steps

  1. Gather the ingredients


  2. Combine flour, sugar, baking powder and salt into a large bowl. Make a well in the center and pour milk, oil and egg. Mix until smooth.


  3. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium-high heat. Pour or scoop batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake; cook until bubbles form and the edges are dry, 1 to 2 minutes. Flip and cook until browned on the other side. Repeat with remaining batter.


  4. Serve hot and enjoy!
